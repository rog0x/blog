Home Cleaning Schedule in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 3 min read · NotionHomeCleaning

A clean home isn't about marathon cleaning sessions — it's about doing small tasks consistently. A Notion cleaning schedule breaks the whole house into 15-20 minute daily tasks so it never gets overwhelming.

The Frequency Framework

  • Daily (10-15 min) — dishes, kitchen wipe-down, make bed, quick floor sweep in high-traffic areas
  • Weekly (1 hour) — vacuum all rooms, mop floors, clean bathrooms, dust surfaces, change towels
  • Biweekly — change bed sheets, clean mirrors, wipe appliances, organize countertops
  • Monthly — deep clean fridge, clean oven, wash windows, vacuum under furniture, clean light fixtures
  • Seasonal — clean gutters, wash curtains, deep carpet clean, organize closets, check smoke detectors

Room-by-Room Approach

Assign each room a deep clean day. Monday: kitchen. Tuesday: bathroom. Wednesday: bedroom. This way you're never cleaning the whole house at once. Each room gets 20-30 minutes of focused attention once a week.

The Notion Advantage

Paper checklists get lost. Apps get abandoned. Notion sits in your workspace where you already are. A "Today's Tasks" view shows just what needs doing right now. Overdue tasks float to the top. Mark done, move on. The system tracks when you last cleaned each thing so you never wonder "has it been 2 weeks or 3 since I mopped?"
