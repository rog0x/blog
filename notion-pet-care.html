Track Your Pet's Health in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 4 min read · NotionPetsHealth

When did your dog get their last rabies shot? What food brand worked best for your cat? How much did you spend on vet bills this year? If you can't answer these instantly, you need a pet tracker.

What to Track

  • Vaccinations — which ones, when, next due date. Bring the list to every vet visit.
  • Medications — daily flea/tick prevention, monthly heartworm. Never miss a dose.
  • Feeding — brand, amount, schedule. When you switch foods, track which ones worked.
  • Weight — monthly weigh-ins catch health problems early.
  • Expenses — food, vet, grooming, toys. Most people underestimate by 40%.
  • Milestones — first walk, learned commands, funny moments. You'll love looking back.

Why Notion

Pet apps exist but they're either too simple (just a vet calendar) or too complex (insurance integration nobody uses). Notion gives you exactly what you need and nothing more.

Plus: share the workspace with your partner/family. Everyone sees the same vet schedule, feeding instructions, and medication reminders. No more "did you give Max his pill?"
