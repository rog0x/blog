Build a Habit Tracker in Notion That Actually Sticks

March 20, 2026 · 5 min read · NotionHabitsProductivity

You've tried habit apps. Habitica, Streaks, Loop, Daylio — downloaded, used for a week, forgotten. The problem isn't willpower. It's friction. Opening a separate app to check off habits feels like work.

But Notion? You're already in it all day. Adding a habit check takes 3 seconds. No extra app, no extra login, no extra subscription.

The Minimum Viable Habit Tracker

Start with the simplest possible system:

  1. One database. Columns: Date, plus one checkbox per habit.
  2. 5-6 habits max. More than that and you'll skip the whole thing.
  3. Check boxes every night. 30 seconds before bed.

That's it. No mood tracking, no journaling, no fancy dashboards. Just checkboxes. Build the habit of tracking habits first.

Adding Streaks

After 2 weeks of consistent tracking, add a formula that counts your current streak. Seeing "Day 14" next to a habit creates a psychological cost to breaking the chain. "I can't break my 14-day streak" is more motivating than "I should exercise."

Adding Mood + Energy

After a month, add two select fields: Mood (Great/Good/OK/Low/Bad) and Energy (High/Medium/Low). Now you can correlate:

  • Do you feel better on days you exercise?
  • Does meditation affect your mood?
  • What habits have the biggest impact on energy?

This turns gut feeling into data.

Weekly Reviews

Every Sunday, 5 minutes:

  1. What went well this week?
  2. What was hard?
  3. What do I adjust for next week?
  4. Three things I'm grateful for.

Over months, these reviews become a journal of personal growth. You can look back at "Week 1: struggled with meditation" and see "Week 12: didn't miss a day" and feel genuinely proud.

The Starter Habits

Don't overthink which habits to track. Start with these 6:

  1. Drink water — 8 glasses
  2. Move — 30 minutes of any exercise
  3. Read — 20 pages or 15 minutes
  4. No phone before 9am — protect your morning
  5. Meditate — even 5 minutes counts
  6. Journal — one sentence before bed

After 30 days, swap out habits that don't resonate. Add new ones that matter to you. The system is yours to customize.

Why This Works

  • Low friction. You're already in Notion. It's one click.
  • Visual progress. Streaks and completion percentages make progress tangible.
  • Connected data. Habits link to mood. Mood links to journal. Everything tells a story.
  • No subscription. $0 beyond what you already pay for Notion.
  • Portable. Works on phone, tablet, laptop. Same data everywhere.
