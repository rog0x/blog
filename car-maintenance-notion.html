Track Car Maintenance in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 3 min read · NotionCarMaintenance

"When was my last oil change?" If you can't answer that immediately, you need a tracker. Notion beats paper records and spreadsheets because it reminds you, calculates MPG, and tracks every dollar.

What to Track

  • Maintenance — oil changes, tires, brakes, battery, inspection. Date, mileage, cost, where. Due dates remind you before it's overdue.
  • Fuel — every fill-up: gallons, cost, mileage. Formula calculates MPG automatically. See trends — if MPG drops, something needs attention.
  • Expenses — everything you spend: maintenance, fuel, insurance, registration, parking. Total cost of ownership per year, per month, per mile.
  • Documents — insurance card photo, registration, warranty info. Pull it up on your phone instead of digging through the glove box.

The MPG Formula

Track odometer at every fill-up. MPG = (current odometer - previous odometer) / gallons filled. Over time, you'll know your car's true efficiency — not the manufacturer's optimistic number.

Cost of Ownership

Most people have no idea what their car costs. Monthly: loan payment + insurance + fuel + maintenance + parking. Per year it's often $8,000-12,000. Knowing the number helps you decide: keep repairing or buy newer?
