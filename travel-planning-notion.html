The Trip Planning System

Before the Trip

  1. Create the trip in your Trip Database. Destination, dates, budget, who's coming.
  2. Research and plan the itinerary day by day. Each activity has a time, location, Google Maps link, and expected cost.
  3. Book everything and log confirmations in the Booking Manager. Flight, hotel, car, tours — every confirmation number in one place.
  4. Set the budget by category. Flights, accommodation, food, activities, transport, shopping. See the total before you leave.
  5. Pack using the packing list. Pre-filled templates for beach, city, business, hiking trips. Check items off as you pack.

During the Trip

  • Open the itinerary on your phone — today's plans with times and locations
  • Log actual expenses in the budget tracker — stay on track
  • Quick access to all booking confirmations when you arrive at the hotel

After the Trip

  • Write in the travel journal — photos, highlights, ratings for restaurants and hotels
  • Compare planned vs actual budget — learn for next time
  • Rate activities and places — your personal travel guide for the future

Packing Lists That Actually Work

The template includes pre-filled packing lists for different trip types. Beach trip? Swimsuit, sunscreen, sandals are pre-checked. Business trip? Suit, laptop charger, business cards. Hiking? Boots, layers, first aid kit.

Duplicate the relevant list for each trip. Check off items as you pack. Never forget your phone charger again.

Budget Without Surprises

Set a budget before you go. Log expenses during the trip. The formulas show remaining budget per category in real-time. If food is 80% spent by day 3 of 7, you know to adjust.
