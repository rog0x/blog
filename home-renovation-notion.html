Plan Your Home Renovation in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 5 min read · NotionRenovationHome

Home renovations go over budget 90% of the time. The ones that don't have one thing in common: a system. Not a spreadsheet you forget to update — a living workspace where every decision, expense, and timeline lives together.

The Renovation System

Room by room. Each room is its own project: before photos, inspiration, measurements, materials, contractor, budget. When you walk into Home Depot, you know exactly what you need for each room.

Budget per category. Labor, materials, permits, fixtures, appliances, contingency (always budget 15-20% contingency — you'll need it). Formulas auto-calculate over/under so you see problems before they become disasters.

Contractor comparison. Get 3 quotes for every trade. Compare them side by side in Notion: price, availability, contract status, reviews. Make decisions with data, not gut feeling.

Material tracking. From "researching" to "ordered" to "delivered" to "installed." Know exactly where every piece of tile, every light fixture, every gallon of paint is in the process. Never discover a missing material on install day.

Decision log. "Why did we pick these countertops?" The decision log captures options considered, what was chosen, and why. When your partner asks 3 months later, you have the answer. When you renovate the next house, you have the playbook.

Why This Saves Money

  • Fewer change orders. Decisions are documented before work starts.
  • Budget visibility. You see over-budget categories before they spiral.
  • Timeline awareness. Dependencies are clear — electrical before drywall, plumbing before tile.
  • Contractor accountability. Contracts, deposits, and scope are tracked.
