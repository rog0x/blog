The Complete Moving Checklist in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 4 min read · NotionMovingChecklist

Moving is the most stressful event after death and divorce, according to research. The stress comes from 200+ tasks spread across 8 weeks with zero margin for error. A structured checklist in Notion turns chaos into a manageable project.

The 8-Week Countdown

  • 8 weeks out — research movers, get quotes, start decluttering. Sell/donate what you won't take.
  • 6 weeks — book movers, start packing rarely-used items, notify landlord.
  • 4 weeks — change address (USPS, banks, subscriptions), transfer utilities, pack room by room.
  • 2 weeks — confirm movers, pack most rooms, clean out fridge/pantry.
  • 1 week — pack essentials bag, defrost freezer, final walkthrough old place.
  • Moving day — supervise movers, check every room, read meters, hand over keys.
  • First week — unpack essentials, update driver's license, meet neighbors.

The Address Change List

This is what people always forget. You need to update your address with: bank, credit cards, insurance (all types), employer, DMV, voter registration, Amazon, subscriptions, doctors, dentist, vet, gym, and about 30 other places. A checklist prevents the "why is my mail still going to my old apartment" problem six months later.

Box Inventory

Number every box. Log contents and destination room. When the movers ask "where does box 47 go?" you can answer "kitchen" without opening it. When you need the coffee maker on day one, you know it's in box 12.

Budget Tracking

Moving costs add up fast: movers ($800-2,000), deposits, cleaning, new furniture, takeout during the move. Track every expense so you know the true cost and can budget for next time.
