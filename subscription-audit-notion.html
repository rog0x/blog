Audit Your Subscriptions, Save $50/Month

March 20, 2026 · 3 min read · NotionFinanceSubscriptions

The average person spends $219/month on subscriptions. Most don't realize it because charges are small and spread across the month. A 30-minute audit in Notion typically saves $20-50/month — that's $240-600/year.

The 30-Minute Audit

  1. Check your credit card/bank statements for the last 3 months
  2. List every recurring charge in Notion
  3. For each: name, cost, billing date, category
  4. Add the cancel URL (google "[service name] cancel subscription")
  5. Rate each: Essential / Nice to Have / Probably Not / Cancel
  6. Cancel everything rated "Cancel" right now
  7. Schedule a quarterly review to repeat

Common Finds

  • Streaming services you don't watch
  • Software trials that auto-renewed
  • Gym membership you haven't used in months
  • Duplicate services (two cloud storage, two music)
  • Apps with free alternatives
