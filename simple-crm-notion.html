The Simplest CRM: Notion

March 20, 2026 · 4 min read · NotionCRMSales

You don't need a CRM. You need a system to remember who you talked to, what you promised, and when to follow up. Notion does that in 5 pages.

The 5-Page CRM

Contacts — name, company, email, type (lead/client/partner). The only fields that matter. Add more when you need them, not before.

Deal Pipeline — kanban board: Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Won/Lost. Drag deals between columns. Total value per column shown at top.

Activity Log — every email, call, meeting logged with one click. When you open a contact, you see every interaction. No more "when did I last talk to them?"

Follow-Up Queue — contacts and deals sorted by urgency. Overdue follow-ups at top. This is your daily action list.

Dashboard — pipeline value, deals this month, win rate. The numbers you need for Monday morning.

The Secret: Follow-Ups

80% of sales happen after the 5th contact. Most people give up after 1-2. The follow-up queue is the most valuable page — it ensures you never forget to reach out.
