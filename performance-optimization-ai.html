Performance Optimization with AI

March 20, 2026 · 6 min read · PerformanceAIOptimization

Most performance problems aren't hard to fix. They're hard to find. AI changes the equation — it can analyze code, estimate complexity, and suggest optimizations in seconds.

The Optimization Framework

Rule #1: Never optimize without measuring. The order matters:

  1. Measure — establish baseline with precise numbers
  2. Profile — find where time is actually spent
  3. Fix — change the bottleneck (not what you guess is slow)
  4. Verify — measure again, confirm improvement
