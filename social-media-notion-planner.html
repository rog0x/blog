Manage Social Media in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 5 min read · NotionSocial MediaMarketing

Buffer costs $6/month per channel. Hootsuite costs $99/month. For planning and tracking content (not scheduling), Notion does it better and for free.

The System

Content Calendar — visual calendar showing every post across all platforms. See the whole week/month at once. Identify gaps. Ensure consistent posting.

Post Database — every piece of content: platform, text, media, hashtags, status, performance metrics. Board view by status (Idea → Draft → Approved → Published) shows your pipeline.

Content Mix — the 40/30/20/10 rule: 40% value (teach), 30% engagement (questions/polls), 20% promotion (your products), 10% personal (behind the scenes). The database tracks your mix automatically.

Hashtag Library — curated sets per platform and niche. Tested and rated. Copy-paste ready. No more last-minute hashtag searching.

Analytics Tracker — weekly: followers gained, engagement rate, top post, worst post. Over months, patterns emerge — which content types, times, and platforms work for you.

What Notion Can't Do

  • Auto-scheduling — you still need Buffer/Later for actual posting
  • Auto-analytics — pull numbers manually (takes 5 min/week)
  • DM management — use native apps for that

Notion handles the planning. Buffer handles the posting. Together they cost $6/month instead of $99.
