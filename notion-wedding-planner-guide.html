Plan Your Wedding in Notion

March 20, 2026 · 5 min read · NotionWeddingPlanning

Wedding planning apps cost $10-30/month. Spreadsheets are ugly. Pinterest boards don't have budgets. Notion does everything — and you probably already have it.

The 8-Page System

Budget Tracker — set budgets per category (venue, catering, photography, flowers). Auto-calculate totals. See exactly where you are vs where you should be. The formula shows over/under for each category. No surprises.

Guest List — every guest with RSVP status, meal choice, plus-one, table assignment, and mailing address. Board view shows attending/declined/no response at a glance. Filter by side (bride/groom) to balance numbers.

Vendor Manager — compare vendors before choosing. Track quotes, deposits, contracts, and remaining balances. Board view by category (venue, catering, photographer) shows your choices at each stage.

Timeline — month-by-month checklist from engagement to honeymoon. Pre-filled with standard wedding planning milestones. Check off as you go. Never miss a deadline.

Seating Chart — assign guests to tables. Track capacity per table. Notes for who can/can't sit together. Linked to guest list so dietary requirements carry over.

Inspiration Board — save ideas by category: decor, dress, flowers, cake. Gallery view with images. Status tracking: idea → considering → chosen. Better than a Pinterest board because it connects to your budget.

Why Notion for Weddings

  • Both partners edit. Share the workspace. Both see real-time updates.
  • Works on phone. Check your guest list at the venue meeting.
  • Everything connects. A vendor links to their budget category. A guest links to their table. A booking links to its cost.
  • No subscription. One-time template purchase vs $30/month for The Knot.
