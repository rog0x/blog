Statuspage.io costs $29/month. Better Uptime costs $20/month. For a simple "is my site up?" check with a public status page, that's overkill.

We built a free alternative. 3 monitors, uptime tracking, response time, incident history. No credit card. No trial that expires.

Why Another Status Page?

Most status page tools are built for enterprises. They have 50 features you don't need and charge accordingly. We built for indie hackers, small teams, and side projects that need a simple answer: "is it up?"

How It Works

Add your URLs. We ping them at regular intervals. If something goes down, it shows on your status page. Your users check the page instead of emailing you "is your site down?"
