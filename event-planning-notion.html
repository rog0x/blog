Whether it's a 30-person birthday party or a 300-person conference, the planning process is the same: guests, vendors, schedule, budget, and a checklist. Notion handles all of it.

The Event System

  • Guest management — RSVP tracking (invited/attending/declined/maybe), meal preferences, plus-ones, table assignments. Board view shows who's in and who hasn't responded.
  • Vendor tracking — compare quotes for catering, venue, entertainment, decorations. Track contracts and payments. Never forget a deposit deadline.
  • Hour-by-hour schedule — setup at 2pm, doors at 5pm, dinner at 7pm, entertainment at 8pm. Everyone sees the same timeline.
  • Budget — planned vs actual per category. See when you're over budget before it's too late.
  • Checklists — 1 month before, 1 week before, day before, day of, after. Never miss "order the cake" or "confirm the DJ".
