AI Prompts for Data Engineering

March 20, 2026 · 4 min read · DataAIPrompts

Data engineering is 80% plumbing. ETL pipelines, schema migrations, SQL optimization, data quality checks — repetitive work that AI accelerates dramatically. The right prompts turn hours of pipeline debugging into minutes.

Data Modeling with AI

Dimensional modeling is an art, but AI can generate a solid first draft. Give it your business requirements and source schemas, and ask for a star schema design. It'll propose fact tables, dimension tables, grain, and relationships. You refine from there.

  • Slowly Changing Dimensions — AI can design SCD Type 1/2/3 implementations based on your business rules. "Customer address changes should be tracked historically" → SCD Type 2 with effective dates.
  • Data Vault — Hub, Link, Satellite design from source schemas. AI maps source tables to vault patterns and generates DDL.
  • Schema Evolution — Plan backward-compatible changes. AI identifies breaking vs non-breaking changes and generates migration scripts.

Pipeline Architecture

Describe your data flow: sources, transformations, destinations, SLAs. AI designs the pipeline architecture: batch vs streaming, incremental vs full load, error handling, retry logic. It writes the skeleton code for Airflow DAGs, dbt models, or Spark jobs.

SQL Optimization

Paste a slow query with the EXPLAIN plan. AI identifies: missing indexes, full table scans, unnecessary joins, subquery anti-patterns, and partition pruning opportunities. It rewrites the query and explains why the new version is faster.

Data Quality at Scale

Data quality is boring until bad data hits production. AI helps design validation frameworks: null checks, referential integrity, range validation, freshness monitoring, anomaly detection. It generates Great Expectations suites or dbt tests from your schema.
